Our Mission

The Empowerment Program is built to raise capitals for idealistic citizens and minimize the rate of proverty in our country. The Program is designed to target US citizens between the ages of 18 and 45 "to acquire and think great ideas on how to be productivity and also contibutes to the welfare of the society." By raising capitals to 200,000 lucky citizens with great ideas on how to start up a business with capital being provided, the Empowerment Program is a community that bring up citizens with great ideas and potential to start up a private owned company.

Our Vision

NPower USA envisions a diverse technology workforce, where people from any cultural ethnicity, gender or socio-economic background can succeed in our digital economy.

How it works

  • Register your name and some vital informations.
  • Send your business strategy to us.
  • You will be get in touch if your business strategy is among those ones choosen
  • You will be ask some futher informations and also partake in some a weekly tutorials.
  • Then will be paid either virtual or physical in any of our branches and then also be monitored and supervise.
